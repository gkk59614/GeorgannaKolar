Ways to Give Gifts
D.I.Y.Buy
Using the Do It Yourself method of gift giving is the best way to save money while giving a very thoughtful gift. Do It Yourself is defined as "the activity of doing or making something (as in woodworking or home repair) without professional training or assistance; broadly : an activity in which one does something oneself or on one's own initiative" (source). Personally, I prefer to give gifts that I make myself for multiple reasons. The main reason is because it shows a person you really know them because of the thought and time that went into the gift. Another reason is because lets be honest, creating D.I.Y. gifts is basically just crafting, and crafting can be a very fun activity. Buying gifts that show you care while still saving money can be very difficult. This is an option that is much better suited for those who don't have a lot of free time to spend creating your own handmade gifts. Ideas for D.I.Y. Presents

  1. If the person you're making a gift for is into southern chic decor, repurposing old wood pallots into wall art is a great way to save big bucks. It is easy to find dirt cheap or even free pallots, and you can buy wood stain and paint for very cheap prices. Depending on the look you are going for, you can either keep the pallot as is, or pull the boards apart and resize them as you please.

  2. If the person you're making a gift for is a big fan of sports, self care, beer, or anything else, you can craft a gift basket full of goodies they are guaranteed to love. For the sports fan, you can create custom cork coasters. For the person who loves self care, you can make a variety of bath bombs and soaps. For the person who loves beer, you can make beer reindeer. The options are endless.
